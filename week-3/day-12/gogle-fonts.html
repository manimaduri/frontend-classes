How Typography Impacts Readability

By Alex Writer - July 2025

Typography is not just about choosing pretty fonts. It's about ensuring readability, setting tone, and helping your content flow. Great font choices and spacing make websites feel elegant and accessible.

“Typography is the craft of endowing human language with a durable visual form.” – Robert Bringhurst

Always combine `font-size`, `font-family`, and `line-height` wisely. Large text with tight line spacing can be uncomfortable. Use `text-align` like `justify` for articles and `center` for headings or quotes.

✅ Tip: Avoid using more than 2–3 font families on a page. Use contrast (like serif + sans-serif) instead of clutter.
